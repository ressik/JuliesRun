Race Details

Details of the race

Each registered participant will receive 1 free raffle ticket and each participant to finish the race will receive a Royal Commemorative Medal. This medal is truly unique to this race and we hope you make it a personal goal to collect one for each year.

Registration opens February 1, 2012 and is limited so register quickly!

All money from registration and the raffle will go directly to the Juvenile Diabetes Research Foundation (JDRF.org). Additional donations are welcome!

5K Details

This will be the most enchanting race you will ever attend.

Participants are encouraged to wear their cape or crown to walk or run the 3.1 miles of winding scenic trails with inspiring and uplifting messages seen all along the way.

The start and finish of this race is at beautiful Barnes Park in Kaysville, Utah.

Kid K (1 K)Details

Kids are encouraged to dress up in their most royal attire.

They will be running on marked trails in shady Barnes Park.

Princesses will be cheering them on with others at the finish line.

Parents are welcome to run with their kids for free.
